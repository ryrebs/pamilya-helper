Weekly House Cleaning Needed

Quezon City Full Time P20,000 - P25,000

Job description

We are in search of a dedicated and reliable individual to provide weekly house cleaning services for our three-bedroom home. This position requires a consistent weekly commitment to maintain a clean and organized living environment. As the house cleaner, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the comfort and tidiness of our home.

Responsibility

  • Perform regular cleaning tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, sweeping, and mopping floors.
  • Clean and sanitize kitchen and bathroom surfaces, including sinks, countertops, and appliances.
  • Make beds and change linens.
  • Empty trash bins and replace liners.
  • Organize and tidy up living spaces, including picking up toys and clutter.
  • Notify the homeowner of any cleaning supplies that need replenishing.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in house cleaning preferred but not mandatory.
  • Attention to detail and thoroughness in cleaning practices.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and respect for the homeowner's privacy.
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to follow instructions.
  • Reliable transportation to and from the job location.
